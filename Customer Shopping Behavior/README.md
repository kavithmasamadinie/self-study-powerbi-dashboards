This Power BI project analyzes consumer shopping habits to uncover trends in customer demographics, purchasing preferences, and sales performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights into what drives customer spending, preferred payment methods, and seasonal trends across various locations.

The analysis is based on the customer_shopping_behavior.csv dataset, which contains detailed records of customer transactions.

Key Data Points:

Customer Demographics: Age, Gender, Location.

Product Details: Category (Clothing, Footwear, etc.), Item Purchased, Size, Color.

Transaction Info: Purchase Amount (USD), Payment Method, Shipping Type.

Customer Engagement: Review Rating, Subscription Status, Frequency of Purchases, Promo Code Usage.

Seasonal Data: Season (Winter, Spring, Summer, Fall).

ðŸ“Š Key Insights & Visualizations
The dashboard aims to answer the following business questions:

1. Demographics & Customer Profile
Gender Distribution: Analyzing purchasing power between Male and Female customers.

Age Groups: Identifying which age groups contribute the most to revenue.

Location Analysis: Visualizing sales performance across different states/regions.

2. Product Performance
Top Selling Categories: Breakdown of sales by categories (e.g., Clothing, Accessories, Footwear).

Popular Items: Identification of the most frequently purchased specific items.

Seasonal Trends: How purchasing behavior shifts during Winter, Spring, Summer, and Fall.

3. Purchase Behavior
Payment Preferences: Analysis of preferred payment methods (Credit Card, PayPal, Cash, etc.).

Shipping Preferences: Breakdown of choices between Express, Free Shipping, Next Day Air, etc.

Impact of Discounts: Analyzing if "Discount Applied" or "Promo Code Used" significantly correlates with higher purchase amounts.

4. Customer Loyalty & Satisfaction
Subscription Impact: Comparing the spending habits of Subscribed vs. Non-Subscribed customers.

Review Ratings: Average customer satisfaction scores by product category.

Retention: Analysis of "Previous Purchases" and "Frequency of Purchases" to identify loyal customer segments.
